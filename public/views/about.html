Our Mission

At the heart of Excelsior Genetics is a legacy of cultivation that spans over twenty years and crosses international borders. Our principal has spent two decades refining his craft in diverse climates and top-tier facilities across the country and abroad, evolving into a master of the plant’s full life cycle. This deep, global perspective allows us to merge traditional techniques with modern horticultural science, ensuring every strain we stabilize is a masterpiece of profile and potency.

This master-level oversight is precisely why we chose the micro-business model. Unlike mass-market producers, we operate a closed-loop system where every plant is treated with artisanal precision. By managing the entire journey from elite seed selection to final harvest, we maintain a standard of quality control that is impossible in industrial-scale operations. For us, it isn’t about volume—it’s about terpene complexity and the transparency our neighbors deserve.

Northern Legacy is the retail manifestation of this expertise. We aren't just opening a shop; we are establishing a destination for craft cannabis in a region we call home. Despite nearly two years of administrative delays and state-level impropriety, our resolve is rooted as deeply as our plants. We remain standing, positioned at the gateway to the 1000 Islands, ready to prove that world-class genetics are worth the wait.
