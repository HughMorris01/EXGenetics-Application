Excelsior Genetics was established in 2023, but our foundation was laid over two decades ago. Our founding principal brings more than 20 years of elite cultivation experience to the brand—a journey that has spanned across the United States and far beyond international borders. This global knowledge wasn’t learned in a boardroom; it was forged in the soil of diverse climates and cultures, mastering the nuances of landrace genetics and modern hybrids alike.

Our mission is to provide top-quality cannabis products by sourcing and perfecting the best genetics in the industry. We believe that every cultivar has a full genetic potential that can only be unlocked through organic, sustainable, and intentional practices. By blending old-world cultivation wisdom with modern environmental stewardship, we produce flower that is as clean as it is potent. For us, excellence is a promise to our partners and the community.

Excelsior Genetics serves as the parent company to Northern Legacy, our upcoming retail arm based in Clayton, NY. As a vertically integrated Microbusiness applicant, we have designed our operation to be a "seed-to-sale" craft powerhouse. This unique license allows us to maintain total control over the entire lifecycle of the plant—allowing us to not only supply our own dispensary but to set the benchmark for high-grade, love-grown cannabis for other licensed retailers across the state.

Despite our deep roots, the transition to the legal market has presented challenges that we refuse to ignore. We are currently engaged in a resolute legal battle with the New York Office of Cannabis Management (OCM) to ensure our application receives the proper and fair review it deserves. We believe that the future of New York’s cannabis industry depends on the inclusion of experienced legacy operators who truly understand the plant. We are standing our ground to ensure that the Excelsior standard has a permanent place in the legal landscape.

We remain steadfast in our pursuit, connected by our love for the cultivars and our commitment to the people who appreciate them. Whether you are a fellow dispensary owner looking for premium wholesale flower or a consumer waiting for our Northern Legacy doors to open, we thank you for your support. While laws may change, true quality is timeless.
